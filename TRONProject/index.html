    Why PrisonCoin

    Wage Disparity

    The prison system exploits prisoners with unfair wages. By leveraging blockchain technology, we can ensure transparency, making wages public and promoting a fair working environment. This system fosters honesty and equity within prisons.

    Transparency

    By leveraging blockchain technology, prisoners' wages can be publicly viewed, ensuring transparency within the prison system and promoting a fair, honest, and accountable working environment for all involved.

    Security

    Blockchain technology ensures transparency by securely tracking all transactions, allowing for comprehensive monitoring of any movement of funds and fostering trust through verifiable, real-time auditing.

    Fast Balance Changes

    Payments are swift and secure. By leveraging advanced payment methods, we eliminate the glitches and errors of the current system, ensuring transactions are instantaneous and reflect immediately.

    Our Mission

    What issues we face

    Wage disparity in prisons is a major issue, with inmates earning extremely low wages for their labor. By using PrisonCoin, we can combat pay disparities and ensure prisoners are paid fairly, resulting in a better working environment.

    A cryptocurrency system could bring transparency, ensuring all payments between prisons and inmates are tracked, reducing exploitation. This would create a fairer, more accountable system for compensating prisoners' work.
