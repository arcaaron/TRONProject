    Why PrisonCoin

    Wage Disparity

    The issue lies within the prison system. Prisoners are taken advantage and paid unfair wages. Issue is, how can we fix this?

    Transparency

    By utilizing the technology of blockchain, all prisoners' wages can be publicly viewed. This ensures prison honesty and promotes fair working environments.

    My Personal Progression

    First Credit Card: Discover It Student Cashback

    The first credit card is one you NEVER want to close and therefore
    I chose the Discover It because of its 5% Cashback rotating categories. I absolutely
    recommend this card to first time users because of its
    low point of entry and people who need a card with the 5% cashback.

    BofA Customized Cash Rewards Credit Card

    This is a great credit card I chose to be a buffer between my
    next choices of credit cards. I use this card specifically for the
    3% cashback on Online Purchases, a feature no other card I have can do. It works well
    for when I order items on the internet. Only issue is BofA can
    deny you of credit card increases and this card can be a pain to keep.

    Chase Freedom Unlimited & Flex

    Chase is the best credit card company that currently exists with
    the Freedom Unlimited and Flex being the two best cards every person needs!
    The Unlimited is a great 1.5% cashback everyday spender's card that allows
    users to rack up cashback rewards on all purchases. The Flex is a great card
    similar to the Discover It that has rotating 5% cashback categories.

    American Express Gold

    The AMEX Gold card was a card I picked up for the sole purpose of being
    the card I used for dining and purchases for food. Since I love food, I
    got this card for the $120 Uber and $120 Seamless annual credit alongside
    many rewards that made this card worth it. Although it now has a hefty annual fee
    of $325, the $240 annual credits + $100 Resy credit makes it worth it in my opinion.
